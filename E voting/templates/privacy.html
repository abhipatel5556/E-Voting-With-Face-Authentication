Privacy Policy for E-Voting System

Your privacy is important to us. This e-voting platform is committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring the confidentiality of your vote.

  • Data Collection: We collect only the information necessary for voter registration, authentication, and election participation.
  • Data Usage: Your personal data is used solely for the purpose of conducting secure and fair elections. We do not share your information with third parties.
  • Vote Confidentiality: All votes are encrypted and stored securely. Your individual vote remains anonymous and cannot be traced back to you.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • User Rights: You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time.
  • Contact: For any privacy-related concerns, please contact the system administrator.

By using this platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
