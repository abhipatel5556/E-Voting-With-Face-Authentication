Administrator Guidance

Welcome to the E-Voting System Admin Guide. This section provides essential instructions and best practices for administrators to ensure secure and efficient election management.

  • Election Creation: Set up new elections by specifying details such as title, candidates, and voting period. Ensure all information is accurate before publishing.
  • Voter Management: Approve voter registrations, monitor voter activity, and resolve any issues related to voter eligibility or access.
  • Security Protocols: Regularly update passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and monitor system logs for suspicious activity.
  • Monitoring & Results: Track election progress in real-time, verify vote counts, and publish results only after thorough validation.
  • Data Privacy: Protect voter data and maintain confidentiality. Follow all privacy guidelines as outlined in the privacy policy.
  • Support: For technical issues or questions, contact the system administrator or support team promptly.

By following these guidelines, administrators help maintain the integrity and trustworthiness of the e-voting process.
